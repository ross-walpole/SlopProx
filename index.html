About AI Slop Filter
🛡️
Keep the internet human.

We built AI Slop Filter because we believe the web is better when it's made by real people — with their quirks, mistakes, and genuine perspectives. AI-generated content at scale dilutes what makes the internet worth visiting.

What it does
✦Detects and hides AI-generated text in articles, feeds, and social media
✦Identifies AI-generated images using a 3-model on-device ensemble
✦Flags YouTube videos where the creator declared AI-generated content
✦Blocks ads and trackers at the network level via a local proxy
✦Runs entirely on your device — no cloud, no tracking, no subscriptions
How it works

A local HTTPS proxy intercepts browser traffic and runs on-device AI models to detect and hide AI content. A companion browser extension extends this to social media feeds (X, Reddit, Instagram, LinkedIn) that need card-level access. All inference stays on your machine — nothing is sent anywhere.

AI Slop Filter v1.9.0 · Local-only · No accounts · No telemetry
Privacy Policy
We collect nothing. Ever.
What we process

Text content and images from pages you visit are analysed locally by on-device machine learning models. This analysis happens entirely on your computer — the results are never transmitted anywhere.

What we don't do
✕Collect or store your browsing data
✕Track which sites you visit
✕Send telemetry or analytics anywhere
✕Operate any servers or cloud infrastructure
✕Sell or share your data with anyone
The local proxy

To filter content on HTTPS pages, AI Slop Filter installs a local Certificate Authority (CA) on your device and runs a local HTTPS proxy on port 8081. This allows the app to read and modify page content before it reaches your browser. This interception is entirely local — no traffic is routed through any external server.

The browser extension

The companion extension communicates only with the local app at 127.0.0.1:8083. It never contacts any external URL and requests no remote permissions.
