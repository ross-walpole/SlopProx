ABOUT SLOPPROX
>_
Keep the internet human.

We built SlopProx because the web is better when it's made by real people — with their quirks, mistakes, and genuine perspectives.

What it does
◈Detects and hides AI-generated text in articles, feeds, and social media
◈Identifies AI-generated images using a 3-model on-device ensemble
◈Flags YouTube videos where the creator declared AI-generated content
◈Blocks ads and trackers at the network level via a local proxy
◈Runs entirely on your device — no cloud, no tracking, no subscriptions
How it works

A local HTTPS proxy intercepts browser traffic and runs on-device AI models to detect and hide AI content. A companion extension extends this to social feeds. All inference stays on your machine.

SlopProx v— · Local-only · No accounts · No telemetry
PRIVACY POLICY
WE COLLECT NOTHING. EVER.
What we process

Text and images from pages you visit are analysed locally by on-device ML models. This never leaves your machine.

What we don't do
✕Collect or store your browsing data
✕Track which sites you visit
✕Send telemetry or analytics anywhere
✕Operate any servers or cloud infrastructure
✕Sell or share your data with anyone
The local proxy

SlopProx installs a local CA and runs a local HTTPS proxy on port 8081. All interception is entirely local — no traffic is routed through any external server.

The browser extension

The companion extension communicates only with the local app at 127.0.0.1:8083. It never contacts any external URL.

TERMS & ATTRIBUTION
Terms of use

SlopProx is provided as-is for personal, non-commercial use. The app modifies your system proxy settings and installs a local CA certificate. Uninstalling restores your original proxy settings.
